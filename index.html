Q.
Can I provide my own design or idea for a custom piece?
A.
You can share a picture of something you like to give us direction and our team will produce something either very unique or similar based on your requirements
Q.
What types of materials do you work with?
A.
We work with a variety of materials which include: Gold, Platinum, Diamonds, Emeralds and Sapphires amongst many others
Q.
How long does it take to create a custom jewelry piece?
A.
The creation process is a few weeks and will reach your doorstep with 4 weeks
Q.
How much does custom jewelry cost?
A.
Our pricing varies based on the materials and craftsmanship required
Q.
Do you offer engraving services?
A.
Yes - this can be done.
Q.
Can you create custom jewelry for special occasions like engagements, weddings, or anniversaries?
A.
We can create all types of jewelry for various occasions
Q.
What types of jewelry do you create?
A.
Our work includes: rings, bracelets, earrings and necklaces
Q.
What is your return or exchange policy for custom-made jewelry?
A.
We do not accept returns unless there is a manufacturer defect. The reason being that each piece is custom made specifically for a person.
Q.
Do you offer repairs or resizing for custom pieces?
A.
Yes, but the jewelry would need to be shipped to Sri Lanka where it is created.
Q.
Do you provide a warranty for custom-made jewelry?
A.
Yes we do. All jewelry is under warranty for the first 6 months after purchase for manufacturing defects.
Q.
Where are your gemstones certified?
A.
Centre stone diamonds are all certified by the GIA
Centre Coloured Gemstones are certified by the Precious Gem Testing Laboratories which are based out of Sri Lanka
Q.
How do I know what my ring size is?
A.
You can find this number out by either going into a physical jewellery store or purchasing a ring sizer on Amazon, which will cost you about $10.

All Celebrate Ceylon Jewellery is under warranty for the first 6 months after purchase for manufacturing defects. This warranty does not protect against pieces that have been recklessly worn or purposely damaged. With regular wear if there are any manufacturing defects, they will typically materialize within the first month or two of use. Repairs needed after 6 months from the day of purchase will be at the expense of the customer along with any shipping costs to Sri Lanka. Pricing for repairs will vary depending on the specific features of the ring. For any ring size changes, any expenses including shipping will be borne by the customer.
